ABOUT EMAGRACE

WE ARE HERE TO HELP YOU.

Emagrace Consultancy was established to support children, young people, and families with high-quality services

Our vision is to empower those involved with social care to achieve meaningful outcomes.

We provide Advocacy, Independent visiting, Mentoring, Training, Consultancy and Recruitment services that empower children, young people, adults, families and organisations to overcome challenges and achieve positive outcomes.

We strive to be recognised for our commitment to safegurading, inclusivity, and innovation while fostering meaningful partnerships with stakeholders and communities.

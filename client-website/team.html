Our Team

At Emagrace Consultancy Limited, our strength lies in the expertise, professionalism, and passion of the people behind our work. We are proud to be led by a diverse team of highly qualified professionals who bring deep experience across health, social care, operations, education, and organisational development. Together, we are committed to delivering excellence, upholding the highest standards, and ensuring every client receives exceptional support.

Leadership Team

Abimbola Adebowale — Director

Abimbola Adebowale is the visionary leader of Emagrace Consultancy Limited. A UK-registered Nurse with over 10 years of experience in the health and social care sector, she brings a wealth of clinical expertise, operational insight, and a strong commitment to quality care.

Her background spans frontline nursing, service management, and strategic consultancy, enabling her to guide organisations with compassion, clarity, and a deep understanding of regulatory expectations.

Abimbola’s leadership is grounded in integrity, safeguarding, and a passion for empowering teams to deliver safe, person-centred services.

Bolaji Adebowale — Operations Lead

Bolaji Adebowale oversees the operational excellence of Emagrace Consultancy Limited. With close to 10 years of experience in the health and social care industry, he brings a unique blend of technical, managerial, and compliance expertise.

His academic achievements include:

  • Engineering degrees
  • A degree in Environmental Management
  • Professional Health & Safety qualifications
  • NVQ Level 7 in Health and Social Care Management

Bolaji’s multidisciplinary background allows him to approach challenges with analytical precision and strategic foresight. He is dedicated to ensuring that our operations, training programmes, and consultancy services consistently meet the highest standards of efficiency, safety, and regulatory compliance.

K. Omotosho — Fire Safety Manager

K. Omotosho is a highly experienced and credentialed fire-risk professional who leads our Fire Risk Assessment service with exceptional technical expertise and regulatory insight.

He holds a Master’s degree in a fire-related discipline, alongside NEBOSH qualifications and recognised fire-risk assessor certification, ensuring his practice meets the highest industry and legislative standards.

With over 20 years of combined experience across Health and Safety, Fire Safety, and Environmental Management, K. Omotosho brings a deep, evidence-based understanding of risk, compliance, and operational safety.

His background spans complex environments where safeguarding, regulatory adherence, and robust risk-mitigation strategies are essential.

He is known for his meticulous approach to assessment, clear communication, and ability to translate technical findings into practical, achievable actions for organisations.

His work supports clients in meeting statutory obligations under the Fire Safety Order 2005, Fire Safety Act 2021, and sector-specific regulations, while strengthening overall safety culture and resilience.

K. Omotosho’s leadership ensures every assessment is thorough, compliant, and tailored to the unique needs of the premises and the people who use it.

Join Our Team

At Emagrace Consultancy Limited, we believe that exceptional care begins with exceptional people. We are always looking for passionate, caring, and dedicated individuals who share our commitment to excellence, safeguarding, and continuous professional development.

If you are driven by purpose, motivated to make a difference, and eager to grow within a supportive and forward-thinking organisation, we would love to hear from you.

Who We’re Looking For

We are seeking committed, skilled, and values-driven professionals who can contribute to the high standards of safety, care, and compliance upheld at Emagrace Consultancy Limited.

Applicants should demonstrate strong leadership qualities, sound judgement, and a deep understanding of regulatory expectations within children’s and health & social care settings.

Essential Qualifications & Professional Requirements

We welcome applications from individuals who are:

  • Qualified to a minimum of Level 5 in Health and Social Care, with progression toward Level 7 or equivalent leadership qualifications strongly preferred
  • Holders of relevant safety and compliance certifications, including:
    • Fire Risk Assessor Certification
    • NEBOSH Health and Safety Management for HSE
    • Fire Risk Assessment (FRA) competency
    • Water Risk Assessment (WRA) competency
  • Experienced, with a minimum of 2 years’ proven practice in HSE, Fire Safety or in health and social care, children’s services, safety compliance, or related operational roles
  • Passionate about delivering high-quality, person-centred support, with a strong commitment to safeguarding and wellbeing
  • Caring, empathetic, and dedicated to improving outcomes for children, young people, and vulnerable individuals
  • Committed to continuous professional development, reflective practice, and maintaining up-to-date knowledge of legislation, standards, and best practice
  • Able to demonstrate alignment with our core values, vision, and mission, contributing positively to a culture of safety, accountability, and excellence
